Common questions

Is a VSO free in Guam?

Yes. Accredited Veterans Service Officers in Guam help you file your VA claim at no charge. You never pay a VSO to prepare or file your claim.

How do I find a VSO near me in Guam?

Use the search above to filter 7 accredited Guam offices by city or name, or tap 'Near me' to sort by distance. You can also use the free locator in the VA Ready app.

What does a VSO do?

A VSO helps you file your claim, gather evidence, becomes your representative (VA Form 21-22), and represents you through appeals — all free. They help you avoid paying a 'claim shark' for help you can get at no cost.
