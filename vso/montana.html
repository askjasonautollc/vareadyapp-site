Common questions

Is a VSO free in Montana?

Yes. Accredited Veterans Service Officers in Montana help you file your VA claim at no charge. You never pay a VSO to prepare or file your claim.

How do I find a VSO near me in Montana?

Use the search above to filter 32 accredited Montana offices by city or name, or tap 'Near me' to sort by distance. You can also use the free locator in the VA Ready app.

What does a VSO do?

A VSO helps you file your claim, gather evidence, becomes your representative (VA Form 21-22), and represents you through appeals — all free. They help you avoid paying a 'claim shark' for help you can get at no cost.
