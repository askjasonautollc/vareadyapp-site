How it works

Setup to filed, five steps.

Five minutes of setup buys a lifetime of benefits you've already earned.

1

Set Up Your Profile

Branch, MOS, service dates, separation date, state, dependents, deployments, and exposure history. Takes 2 minutes. Everything stays on your device.

2

Build Your Claim

Pick from the most-claimed conditions or search all 749. Rate each one using the VA's own criteria. Watch your combined rating update in real-time.

3

Open Claim Intel

One screen merging your MOS-typical conditions, PACT Act presumptives, and conditions tied to your exposures. Tap to add. The app warns you about pyramiding before it lands. Multi-MOS supported.

4

Track Your Evidence

For each condition: diagnosis, service records, nexus letter, buddy statements, DBQ review. Check them off as you gather them. Know when you're ready.

5

File With Confidence

Export your claim summary with every condition, diagnostic code, and estimated rating. Use it as your checklist on VA.gov. Nothing missed, nothing forgotten.
