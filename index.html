How It Works

From Setup to Filing in Minutes

1

Set Up Your Profile

Enter your branch, MOS, service dates, separation date, state, and deployment history. Takes 2 minutes. Everything stays on your device.

2

Build Your Claim List

Add conditions from the most-claimed list or search all 730. Select your estimated rating or use the full VA criteria (Pro) to match your symptoms to the right level.

3

See Your Combined Rating

Watch the VA math engine calculate your combined rating in real-time with step-by-step breakdown, bilateral factor, and monthly compensation estimate.

4

Track Your Evidence

Check off each piece of evidence as you gather it — diagnosis, service records, nexus letters, buddy statements. Know when you're ready to file.

5

Export and File

Export your claim summary with all conditions, diagnostic codes, and estimated ratings. Use it as your checklist when filing on VA.gov.
