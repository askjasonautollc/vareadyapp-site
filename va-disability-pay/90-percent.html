How VA pays your rating

VA pays compensation in 10% steps from 10% to 100%, and the amount is the same nationwide. If you have multiple conditions, VA combines them with its own math (not simple addition) and rounds to the nearest 10% — so a combined 75% pays at the 80% rate. Common questions

How much is 90% VA disability per month in 2026?

90% VA disability pays $2,362.30 per month for a veteran with no dependents, effective December 1, 2025. With dependents it is higher — for example $2,559.30 with a spouse.

Is VA disability compensation taxable?

No. VA disability compensation is not taxed by the federal government or by states.

When did these rates take effect?

These are the 2026 rates, effective December 1, 2025. VA adjusts them each year for cost of living (COLA).

Does 90% VA disability include money for a spouse and kids?

Yes. At 90% VA adds dependent amounts — $2,559.30 with a spouse, $2,704.30 with a spouse and one child, plus $98.00 for each additional child under 18.
