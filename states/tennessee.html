Property Tax

  1. Property Tax Exemption100% P&T
    Est. $2,500/yr · Tax relief on first $175,000 market value — typical reimbursement ~$1,500–$3,500/yr depending on county tax rate

    Tennessee Disabled Veterans Property Tax Relief. NOT an exemption — the state REIMBURSES county trustees for the property tax paid by qualifying disabled veterans on the first $175,000 of the home's market value. NO income limit (unlike the senior/disabled-homeowner relief tier). Eligibility: honorable (or other-than-dishonorable) discharge AND a VA-rated 100% permanent and total service-connected disability OR paraplegia/paid at 100% TDIU/loss of two or more limbs/legal blindness from service. Year 1 you pay the bill in full and submit proof to receive a state refund; in subsequent years you receive a voucher that pre-reduces the bill. Surviving unremarried spouse continues the relief. Apply at the County Trustee.

    How to claim: Apply at your County Trustee with VA P&T disability award letter; year 1 pay first then submit proof for state refund

Tennessee offers veteran benefits across property tax, income tax, vehicle. Highlights include Property Tax Exemption, No State Income Tax, Free License Plate. Eligibility varies — some benefits require a VA disability rating, 100% P&T status, or combat service.
