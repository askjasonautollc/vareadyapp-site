Description of Service

VA Ready is an informational tool that helps veterans:

  • Estimate their combined VA disability rating using VA math
  • Explore federal and state veteran benefits
  • Review conditions common to their military occupational specialty
  • Understand the VA claims filing process

The App is a reference tool only. It does not file claims, access VA systems, or interact with any government database on your behalf.
