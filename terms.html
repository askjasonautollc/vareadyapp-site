Description of Service

The App is an informational tool that helps veterans:

  • Estimate their combined VA disability rating using VA math, including TDIU (Total Disability Individual Unemployability) and P&T (Permanent & Total) calculations
  • Surface conditions and presumptive pathways based on their military occupational specialty (MOS), duty assignments, and documented exposures — cross-referenced against the PACT Act, Camp Lejeune, AFFF / PFAS, K2 (Karshi-Khanabad), Agent Orange, and other registries (the "Exposure Calculator")
  • Explore federal and state veteran benefits at any rating level
  • Read CFR-sourced filing guides and VA form explainers for commonly-claimed conditions, including DBQ references, rating criteria, and evidence checklists
  • Browse references and links to third-party, peer-reviewed medical literature related to commonly-claimed conditions
  • Find and contact VA-accredited representatives, and read or contribute ratings and reviews of those offices
  • Track symptoms over time and export a personal summary or claim document
  • Schedule local reminders for C&P exams, appeal deadlines, and other claim milestones
  • Understand the VA claims filing process
  • Submit feedback, feature requests, and bug reports to the developer, optionally including a reply-to email address

The App is a reference tool only. It does not file claims, access VA systems, or interact with any government database on your behalf.
